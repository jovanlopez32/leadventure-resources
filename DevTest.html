The History of Schrock Repair

Schrock Repair is located at 1422 U.S. Highway 150 in Goodfield, IL as an automotive repair, tire, and wheel center. We have spent the last 11 years delivering auto repairs, tires, and wheels to satisfied customers. You can come see us in person, or schedule an appointment online today from the comfort of your couch.

Jeff Schrock opened Schrock Repair in 2003 and moved it into the current location in 2005. We bring our mechanical expertise to repairs for cars, trucks, RVs, boats, and trailers. With more than 120 years of combined experience, our staff takes pride in having the knowledge needed to provide you with reliable service that you can trust. We also have the facilities and services needed to store your equipment.
