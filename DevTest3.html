SIGNS THAT IT’S TIME TO SCHEDULE A

TIRE SERVICE WITH KITTS AUTO SERVICE INC

As a responsible vehicle owner, it’s crucial to stay vigilant for signs that indicate your tires require professional servicing. Regularly monitoring the condition of your tires can help you maintain optimal performance, maximize their lifespan, and ensure your safety on the road.

Kitts Auto Service Inc recommends paying attention to the following signs that may indicate the need for tire service:

  • Prolonged Loss of Tire Pressure:

    Consistent loss of tire pressure indicates a slow leak or puncture.

  • Excessive Road Noise:

    Unusual road noise can signify tire wear, improper inflation, or damage.

  • Uneven Tread Wear:

    Check for uneven tread wear, which may indicate misalignment or improper inflation.

  • Bulges or Blisters:

    Bulges or blisters on tire sidewalls signal internal damage and weakened integrity.

  • Low Tread Depth:

    Worn-out tread poses a safety hazard, especially in wet conditions.

  • Vibration or Pulling:

    Steering wheel vibrations or vehicle pulling may indicate tire balance, alignment, or suspension issues.
