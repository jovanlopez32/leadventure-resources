About Razorback Camper Sales
54 Years and Counting! Serving Hot Springs, Little Rock, Conway, Cabot, Mena, Fort Smith, Texarkana, Benton, Pine Bluff, Malvern, Arkadelphia, Searcy, Russellville and everywhere in between!

Razorback Camper Sales was founded in 1969 by C.L. Johnson in Hot Springs, Arkansas. Three generations later, Razorback Camper Sales is still owned and operated by the Johnson Family making it the oldest “Family-Owned” RV dealership in the state. Our dealership sells fifth wheels, travel trailers and pop-ups, offers a 5-star service department and has the area's largest parts store. We pride ourselves in taking care of the customers before and after the sale.

Razorback Camper Sales continues to be a leader in the RV industry. We firmly believe when buying a camper, it is important to realize you are buying a dealership…. sales, parts, and service. Our dealership takes satisfaction in being knowledgeable in all realms of the business while providing the best customer service possible.
