Our Check Engine Light

Diagnosis and Service Process

At X-Treme Performance Inc, we take pride in our comprehensive check engine light diagnosis and service process. Here’s how we do it:

1

Step 1: Diagnosis

Our experienced mechanics use advanced scanning tools to read the diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs) stored in your vehicle’s computer system. We then analyze the data to identify the root cause of the problem.

2

Step 2: Repair

Once we identify the issue, we proceed to repair it according to the manufacturer’s specifications. We only use genuine parts and ensure that all repairs are carried out to the highest standards.

3

Step 3: Testing

After repairing the problem, we carry out a series of tests to ensure that your car is functioning optimally. We test everything from the engine performance to the emissions system to ensure that your car is roadworthy and safe.
