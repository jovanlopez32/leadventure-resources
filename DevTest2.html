Veterans / Active Duty Package

As Oregon's premier RV dealer, RV Outlet in Eugene can help our northern neighbors in Canada purchase their RV and save money! The following are some steps to make this an easy process for you.

The first thing we do is come to an agreement on a price and receive a deposit to hold the RV. Then we collect the information necessary to complete the purchase. For a trade-in, we are happy to walk you through the import process. Some customers prefer to use a broker to assist with the customer's paperwork. We can help you find one so you don't feel lost as to where to look.

After that has been done and the sale has been finalized, you may head on down the road with your RV. We will furnish you with the title, odometer disclosure, and recall letter for you and the border office as well as all other required documentation for ownership transfer in Canada. Be sure to check if your province requires equipment such as engine block heaters, daytime running lights, etc., be sure to inform us. We are happy to help in making the modifications to your RV so it is compliant.

If you have any questions about this information or an RV for sale, please call (541) 688-0310 or email us at sales@rvoutletonline.com and we will be happy to help and show you why RV Outlet of Eugene is the best place to purchase your next RV!
