Jones Service Station has been providing quality car care in Olive Branch, Illinois, since 1948. We are a family-owned business and a one-stop-shop delivering honest and professional automotive repair and auto maintenance services to the people of Olive Branch and surrounding areas. The quality ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certified technicians at Jones Service Station employ today’s latest automotive technology and are equipped to handle all major and minor repairs on foreign and domestic vehicles.
